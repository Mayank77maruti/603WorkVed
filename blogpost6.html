Getting Started with 603's Space Calculator

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on using 603 The Coworking Space's advanced office furniture budget calculator and space planning tools. Our platform offers powerful features to help you design and budget for your perfect workspace, whether you're setting up a small startup office or planning a large corporate space.

Step 1: Choose Your Layout Style

Start your journey by visiting our "Make Your Space" section. You'll find four distinct layout options:

Combination Layout

Perfect blend of open spaces and private areas. Ideal for teams that need both collaboration zones and focused work areas.

Coworking Layout

Flexible shared workspace design. Great for startups, freelancers, and small businesses looking for community-driven environments.

Minimal Layout

Clean, uncluttered design focusing on essential furniture. Perfect for businesses prioritizing simplicity and efficiency.

Open Plan Layout

Spacious, barrier-free design promoting collaboration. Ideal for creative teams and modern corporate environments.

Step 2: Using the Budget Calculator (BOQ)

Once you've selected your preferred layout, you'll access our comprehensive Budget Calculator (Bill of Quantities). This powerful tool helps you estimate costs across different budget ranges.

Calculator Categories

Cubicles and Partitions
  • Small Cubicle (4'x2'): Compact workstations for individual tasks
  • Standard Cubicle (5'x6'): Balanced workspace for most office needs
  • Large Cubicle (8'x8'): Spacious workstations for senior staff or collaborative work
Office, Conference & Reception Furniture
  • Executive Office (14'x20'): Premium private offices for leadership
  • Large Office (11'x16'): Spacious private offices for managers
  • Small Office (9'x9'): Compact private offices for individual use
  • Conference Rooms: Small (11'x15') and Large (14'x20') meeting spaces

Step 3: Operating the Calculator

1

Enter Quantities

Input the number of each furniture type you need in the quantity fields. The calculator accepts whole numbers only.

2

View Square Footage

The system automatically calculates the minimum square footage required based on your selections.

3

Compare Budget Options

Review three pricing tiers:

  • Economy: Budget-friendly options without compromising quality
  • Mid-Range: Perfect balance of style and affordability
  • High-End: Premium furniture with luxury finishes
4

Review Totals

Check the subtotals for each category and the grand total for your entire project across all budget ranges.

Step 4: Additional Calculator Features

Quick Contact Integration

The calculator includes a Quick Contact Form where you can specify your furniture interests and quantities. This helps our team provide personalized recommendations.

Visual Previews

Each furniture category includes thumbnail images. Click on these previews to see larger images and better understand the furniture styles available.

Expandable Sections

The calculator organizes furniture into collapsible sections. Click the chevron icons to expand or collapse categories for easier navigation.

Pro Tips for Using Our Calculator

Plan for Growth

Consider adding 10-20% extra capacity to accommodate future expansion without major redesigns.

Mix Budget Ranges

You don't have to stick to one budget tier. Mix economy and mid-range options to optimize your investment.

Consider Space Flow

Pay attention to the square footage calculations to ensure proper circulation and comfort in your space.

Consult Our Experts

Use the Quick Contact Form or call us directly for personalized advice on your space planning needs.
